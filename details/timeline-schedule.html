Schedule#

Note

Although the schedule is almost set, we will still make some adjustments to it, so please check back!

All CI2023 Reproducibility Challenge activities will take place virtually on May 1-31. The Zoom link is pinned on the #6-reproducibility-challenge channel in the CI2023 slack workspace. We will have an overview of technologies, followed by a series of talk with the expert sessions. We strongly encourage participants to attend these sessions. Clinics & Coffee Drop-in OPTIONAL activities will be on Fridays 15:00-16:00. See the schedule below for details.

All times listed below are UTC + 1 (British Summer Time). You might want to consult this Time Zone Map to figure out times in your location.

We suggest teams allocate an average of about 2 hours per day for preparing their submissions.

The schedule is also available as a Google Calendar (see here).
